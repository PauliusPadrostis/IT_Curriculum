# I1 (1) — Mini workflow: compute → sort/filter → interpret

**Type:** 🔗 Integration
**Grade:** Grade 10 (II gimnazijos klasė)
**Module:** Data – Spreadsheet Foundations
**Semester:** Semester 1
**Status:** 🚧 WIP

---

## Context

- Given dataset: compute derived columns (e.g., totals, averages)
- Apply sort/filter to answer a question
- Write 2–3 sentence conclusion based on evidence.

## Completion Definition

Student executes a full micro-analysis cycle: compute derived values, apply sorting/filtering aligned with a question, and produce a short interpretation grounded in the results.

## Minimum Evidence

Given a dataset, student:\
creates at least one computed column\
applies sorting or multi-criteria filtering correctly\
identifies a meaningful pattern or comparison\
writes a 2–3 sentence interpretation referencing specific values.

## Cognitive Level (Bloom's)

Apply → Analyze (procedural execution followed by structured interpretation).

## Readiness Gate

Interpretation must reference computed results (not vague observations) and remain logically consistent with the filtered/sorted subset.
