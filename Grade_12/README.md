# Grade 12 – Advanced Data · Advanced Programming · VBE Exam Prep

**Year focus:** Exam-grade mastery — multi-step data reporting, file-based programming with sorting and searching algorithms, and sustained VBE mock practice under timed conditions.

## Modules

| Semester | Module | Topic |
|----------|--------|-------|
| 1 | [01_Data_Reporting](Semester_1/01_Data_Reporting/) | Multi-criteria filtering, pivot reports, chart selection, switching techniques |
| 1 | [02_AI_Practical](Semester_1/02_AI_Practical/) | Classification and prediction with Orange Data Mining (GUI-based ML) |

## Progression logic

Semester 1 consolidates both data and programming at exam level; every task mirrors VBE task structure and marking criteria. AI Practical and E-Publishing Refresher modules round out applied skills. Semester 2 adds sorting/searching, then shifts entirely to exam simulation — mocks, targeted error clinics, and pacing drills. Theory Load packs are distributed across both semesters and integrated into Exam Prep timed sessions.

## Assessment points

- Practical assessments in every module
- Timed tasks in Module 06 (VBE format — practical + theory)
- Three mock cycles (lessons 113–121)
- Error clinics and diagnostic rotation drills (lessons 122–125)
- Exam Prep module includes theory drilling from Theory Load packs (GDPR, e-signatures, IoT/5G, troubleshooting, digital empathy)
