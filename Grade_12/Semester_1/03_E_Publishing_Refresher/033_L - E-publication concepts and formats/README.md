# L — E-publication concepts and formats

**Type:** 📖 Lesson
**Grade:** Grade 12
**Module:** E-Publishing Refresher
**Semester:** Semester 1
**Status:** 🚧 WIP
**Sequence:** 33 of grade year

---

## Overview

- What counts as an electronic publication
- Publication lifecycle (design → produce → publish → distribute)
- Formats (ePub, PDF, web)
- CMS tools (WordPress, BookCreator)
- Multimedia integration (graphics, audio, video, animation in publications)

## Sub-topics

- Electronic publication definition and types
- Publication lifecycle stages
- File formats for digital publishing
- Content management systems
- Multimedia in publications

---

## 1️⃣ Completion Definition

Student can describe the e-publication lifecycle, name common formats, and explain how multimedia enhances digital publications.

## 2️⃣ Minimum Evidence

Correctly identifies publication formats and describes at least 3 stages of the publication lifecycle.

## 3️⃣ Bloom's Taxonomy Level

Level 2 — Understand

## 4️⃣ Readiness Gate

None (refresher of Grade 9 concepts).

## 5️⃣ Common Failure Modes

- Confusing file formats (ePub vs PDF capabilities)
- Not understanding the full lifecycle beyond "create and publish"
- Thinking digital publishing = just making a PDF
