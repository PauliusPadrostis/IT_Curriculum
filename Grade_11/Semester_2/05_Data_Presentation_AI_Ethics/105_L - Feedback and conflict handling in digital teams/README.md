# L105 — Feedback and conflict handling in digital teams

**Type:** 📖 Lesson
**Grade:** Grade 11
**Module:** Data – Presentation, AI & Ethics
**Semester:** Semester 2
**Sequence:** 105 of grade year
**Status:** 🚧 WIP

## Overview

Structured critique, issue escalation, accountability.\
\

---

## 1️⃣ Completion Definition

Student can apply basic feedback structures (specific, behavior-focused, actionable) and propose appropriate steps for resolving common digital-team conflicts (miscommunication, missed deadlines, unclear ownership).

## 2️⃣ Minimum Evidence

Given 3 conflict scenarios, student writes:\
One feedback message using a structured format (e.g., situation–behavior–impact–request)\
One resolution plan with at least 3 steps (clarify facts, align on contract, agree next actions)

## 3️⃣ Bloom's Taxonomy Level

Apply → Evaluate

## 4️⃣ Readiness Gate

Does the student’s response avoid blame/assumptions and instead propose verifiable steps and agreements? Yes/No
