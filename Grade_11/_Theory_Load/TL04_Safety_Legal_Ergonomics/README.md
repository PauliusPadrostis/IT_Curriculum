## Key Concepts Students Must Know

- Legal/ethical framework for IT use (what's allowed, what's not)
- Account and device safety in school/work context
- Health and ergonomics: proper workstation setup, break patterns
- Environmental impact of digital technologies
- Data protection basics (GDPR awareness)

## Exam Relevance

1–2 questions in VBE Part 1 on safety practices, legal requirements, and ergonomics.
