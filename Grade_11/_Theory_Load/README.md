# Grade 11 — Theory Load Packs

Theory Load packs deliver curriculum-required theory content as structured homework. Students read the material independently and complete a self-check quiz. In-class time is reserved for practical application (I lessons) and formal assessment (A lessons).

## How It Works

1. **Teacher assigns** a Theory Load pack via Google Classroom at the specified point in the semester
2. **Students read** the PDF/Canva presentation and complete the self-check quiz
3. **In-class sessions** (I/A lessons in the relevant module) assume students have read the material
4. **Formal assessment** happens in Module 10 (Theory Assessment) — two graded quizzes covering all theory-loaded content
