# Canva export checklist for Safety pilot

## General checks
- Confirm every generated slide uses the intended template type.
- Confirm visible text does not contain lesson codes or internal file names.
- Confirm no placeholder names are still visible.
- Confirm text is readable from projector distance.
- Confirm line breaks do not create cramped blocks.
- Confirm all quotes are straight double quotes only.
- Confirm no em dashes appear in visible text.
- Confirm every must-show slide still exists after layout adjustments.

## Lesson-specific checks

### 001_L
- Slide 3 must clearly contrast correct and incorrect posture.
- Slide 4 must keep the numbers in 20-20-20 visually obvious.

### 002_L
- Slide 3 must show strong vs weak password logic immediately.
- Slide 4 must read as a process, not as two unrelated text boxes.
- Slide 6 fake message must look suspicious but still readable.

### 003_L
- Slide 3 must not become a text wall.
- Slide 4 arrow order must stay clear: SUSTOTI -> PATIKRINTI -> PRANEŠTI.
- Slide 5 must visually mark the suspicious sender, urgency, and link.

### 004_L
- Slide 3 must support the lecture block, not just summarize it.
- Slide 4 must make the high vs low resource contrast obvious.
- Slide 5 must keep Lithuanian examples concrete and local.

### 005_I
- Slide 3 must remain visible during task explanation.
- Slide 4 worked example must clearly separate scenario from analysis.
- Slide 5 must help students avoid shallow answers.

## Export
- Export lesson sets as PDF after the visual check.
- Keep the Canva source reusable for the next module batch.
