# Asset prompts for Safety Canva pilot

Create reusable assets, not decorative one-offs.

## 001_L
- Correct vs incorrect workstation setup
  Prompt: Flat educational comparison showing one correct seated posture and one incorrect posture, student at desk, visible screen height and foot support, classroom-safe style.
- Eye break and rest rhythm
  Prompt: Minimal process visual for 20-20-20 and regular breaks, clock plus eye icon, high contrast.

## 002_L
- Strong vs weak password comparison
  Prompt: Two-card comparison for password examples, one clearly strong, one clearly weak, readable from projector distance.
- 2FA flow
  Prompt: Two-step login flow, password then phone or app confirmation, simple arrows and icons.
- Suspicious password-request message
  Prompt: Fake message card asking for a password, clean warning highlight zones, no realistic branding.

## 003_L
- Phishing signs board
  Prompt: Educational board with six phishing signs, warning markers, suspicious email cues, clean non-scary style.
- SUSTOTI -> PATIKRINTI -> PRANEŠTI flow
  Prompt: Bold three-step classroom flow with arrows and large Lithuanian labels.
- Fake phishing email mockup
  Prompt: Example phishing email with highlighted sender, urgency, suspicious link, and call to action.

## 004_L
- Negative vs positive impact comparison
  Prompt: Split comparison with energy use and e-atliekos on one side, environmental monitoring and maps on the other.
- Daily digital actions by resource use
  Prompt: Comparison cards for video stream, email with attachment, cloud storage, and text message, clean icons and strong contrast.
- Lithuania environmental tools set
  Prompt: Three-card style showing monitoring data, map-based viewing, and reporting platform actions.

## 005_I
- Three-step scenario analysis frame
  Prompt: Reusable problema-taisyklė-veiksmas frame for classroom analysis, minimal structure, large labels.
- Worked example scenario card
  Prompt: One scenario card plus analysis blocks, clean teacher-led demonstration layout.

## Reuse note
- If an asset works across lessons, create it once and swap only text layers inside Canva.
