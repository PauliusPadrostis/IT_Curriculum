# L25 — Working with images in layout

**Type:** 📖 Lesson
**Grade:** Grade 9
**Module:** Specialisation: Digital Printing
**Semester:** Semester 2
**Sequence:** 25 of grade year
**Status:** 🚧 WIP

---

## Overview

1️⃣ Completion Definition\
Student places and adjusts images in a print layout while maintaining quality and correct positioning (cropping, scaling, alignment, captions if needed).

---

## 1️⃣ Completion Definition

Student can explain working with images in layout.

## 2️⃣ Minimum Evidence

Layout draft with at least two images, with documented crop/scale decisions and a short quality check (no pixelation for intended print size).

## 3️⃣ Bloom's Taxonomy Level

Apply → Analyze

## 4️⃣ Readiness Gate

Student can identify when an image is insufficient quality for print and chooses an appropriate fix (replace, rescan, resize).
