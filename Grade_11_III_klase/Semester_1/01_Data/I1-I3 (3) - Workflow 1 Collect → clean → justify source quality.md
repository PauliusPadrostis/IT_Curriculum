# I1–I3 (3) — Workflow 1: Collect → clean → justify source quality

**Type:** 🔗 Integration
**Grade:** Grade 11 (III gimnazijos klasė)
**Module:** Data – Collection, Joins & Databases
**Semester:** Semester 1
**Status:** 🚧 WIP

---

## Context

Students build a small dataset and document provenance + cleaning decisions.\
\

## Completion Definition

Student executes a full workflow:

- selects data

- performs basic cleaning (missing values, duplicates, format issues)

- documents what was cleaned and why

- justifies source quality and implications

## Minimum Evidence

Submission includes:

- before/after dataset (or clearly shown changes)

- a “cleaning log” (bullets: what changed + why)

- reliability justification (condensed version)

- final short conclusion: what can/can’t be claimed

## Cognitive Level (Bloom's)

**Bloom:** Apply → Analyze

## Readiness Gate

✔ All true:

- cleaning changes are real and explained

- no silent deletion without justification

- conclusion matches what data supports\
  If not → not ready for P1/A1.
