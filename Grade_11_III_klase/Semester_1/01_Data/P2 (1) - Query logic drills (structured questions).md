## Context

## Completion Definition

Student answers structured logic questions about:

- which operation is needed (join/filter/group)

- what result a step would produce

- what error a wrong step introduces

## Minimum Evidence

- ≥3 structured logic items

- ≥1 “spot the mismatch” item

## Cognitive Level (Bloom's)

- identifies at least one mismatch consequence correctly\
