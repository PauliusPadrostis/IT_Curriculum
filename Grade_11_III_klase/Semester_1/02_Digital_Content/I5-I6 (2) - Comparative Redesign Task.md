## Context

**Workflow**

- Given a poorly designed visual → redesign + explain improvements

- Students identify manipulation or structural flaws

**This strengthens analytical skill needed for VBE 1.**\
\

## Completion Definition

Student compares two visual designs (original + improved) and explains:

- what was weak

- what was improved

- how clarity/ethics improved

## Minimum Evidence

- Before/after visuals

- 5–8 sentence analytical comparison

## Cognitive Level (Bloom's)

Analyze → Evaluate

## Readiness Gate

✔ Identifies specific weaknesses\
✔ Improvements tied to design principles
