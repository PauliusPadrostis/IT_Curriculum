# L9–L10 (2) — Visual communication ethics

**Type:** 📖 Lesson
**Grade:** Grade 11 (III gimnazijos klasė)
**Module:** Digital Content – Vector Graphics & Publications
**Semester:** Semester 1
**Status:** 🚧 WIP

---

## Context

**Supports VBE 1 interpretative reasoning.**\
\

## Sub-topics

- Manipulation via scale, cropping, selective display

- Misleading infographics and distortion

- Responsible data communication

## Completion Definition

Student can:

- Identify manipulation risks in visual communication

- Explain how design choices can mislead (scale, color emphasis, omission)

- Apply an ethical lens to visual presentation

## Minimum Evidence

Student analyzes one flawed visual and:

- identifies ≥2 manipulation risks

- explains potential impact on audience

- proposes corrections

## Cognitive Level (Bloom's)

**Bloom:** Analyze → Evaluate

## Readiness Gate

✔ All true:

- Risks identified are real (not vague)

- Explanation links design choice to audience impact

- Proposed correction improves clarity\
  If not → not ready for integration projects.
