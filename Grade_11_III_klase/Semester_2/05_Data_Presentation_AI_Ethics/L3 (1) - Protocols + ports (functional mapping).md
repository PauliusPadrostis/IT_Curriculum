# L3 (1) — Protocols + ports (functional mapping)

**Type:** 📖 Lesson
**Grade:** Grade 11 (III gimnazijos klasė)
**Module:** Data – Presentation, AI & Ethics
**Semester:** Semester 2
**Status:** 🚧 WIP

---

## Context

DNS, HTTP/HTTPS, DHCP, TCP vs UDP; “which protocol explains this symptom?”\
\

## Completion Definition

Student can match common protocols (HTTP, HTTPS, FTP, SMTP, DNS, etc.) to their purpose and explain the role of ports in enabling service multiplexing.

## Minimum Evidence

Given 6 service scenarios, student selects the appropriate protocol and explains its function; student explains why ports are needed when multiple services run on one host.

## Cognitive Level (Bloom's)

Understand → Analyze

## Readiness Gate

Can the student explain protocol selection based on function rather than memorized port numbers? Yes/No
