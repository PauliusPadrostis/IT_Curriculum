## Overview

Team gets a mini-task: plan, assign, communicate, deliver within constraints; traceable evidence required.\
\

---

## 1️⃣ Completion Definition

Student coordinates a short team simulation by applying a chosen collaboration model, enforcing role responsibilities, using agreed tool discipline, and producing traceable coordination outputs.

## 2️⃣ Minimum Evidence

Simulation artifacts include:\
A task board or action list with owners and deadlines\
A decision log or meeting note with outcomes\
A documented handoff (what is delivered, to whom, and acceptance criteria)

## 3️⃣ Bloom's Taxonomy Level

Apply → Analyze

## 4️⃣ Readiness Gate

Are coordination artifacts complete enough that an absent team member could reliably continue work? Yes/No
