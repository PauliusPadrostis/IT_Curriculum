# L2 (1) — Roles, responsibilities, and workflow contracts

**Type:** 📖 Lesson
**Grade:** Grade 11 (III gimnazijos klasė)
**Module:** Virtual Communications
**Semester:** Semester 2
**Status:** 🚧 WIP

---

## Context

Who decides, who reviews, who merges; definition-of-done.\
\

## Completion Definition

Student can define roles and responsibilities in a digital team (owner, contributor, reviewer, coordinator) and can explain how “workflow contracts” reduce errors (handoffs, definition of done, deadlines, approval paths).

## Minimum Evidence

Student produces a simple workflow contract for a mini-project that includes: role assignments, handoff rules, definition of done, and one escalation rule for delays or blockers.

## Cognitive Level (Bloom's)

Apply → Analyze

## Readiness Gate

Is the contract operational (actionable rules) rather than vague (“work together better”)? Yes/No
