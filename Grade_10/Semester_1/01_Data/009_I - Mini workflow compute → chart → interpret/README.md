# I9 — Mini workflow: compute → chart → interpret

**Type:** 🔗 Integration practice
**Grade:** Grade 10
**Module:** Data – Spreadsheet Foundations
**Semester:** Semester 1
**Sequence:** 9 of grade year
**Status:** 🚧 WIP

---

## Overview

- Create chart that answers a question
- Add labels and short conclusion (“what does it show?”).

---

## 1️⃣ Completion Definition

Student performs a complete micro-cycle of analysis: computes derived values, selects and constructs an appropriate chart, and formulates a concise interpretation grounded in the visualized results.

## 2️⃣ Minimum Evidence

Given a dataset, student:\
creates at least one computed metric\
constructs a chart aligned with the analytical question\
ensures axes, labels, and legend are correct\
writes a 2–3 sentence interpretation referencing specific values or visible trends in the chart.

## 3️⃣ Bloom's Taxonomy Level

Apply → Analyze (integration of numeric computation and visual reasoning).

## 4️⃣ Readiness Gate

Interpretation must reference actual chart evidence (trend, comparison, proportion) and remain logically consistent with the computed values.
